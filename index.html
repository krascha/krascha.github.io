Hey my name is...

...Liz and i am an industrial design student at the HTW in Berlin. I decided for industrial design, because i was grown up in a crafted family. It was also normal for me, that i was always surrounded by a lot of animals, there my grandpa had more than the usual pets. The grandparents lived in a village and he had a little bird breeding there. I learned to be responsible, that we have got a sustainable for coexisting with animals and nature.

I am always looking for new challenges and I like to learn new things from different fields, so I am also interested in photography, layout. I started programming a year ago, besides studying.
